Work Signals

A practical guide to navigating the modern workplace with clarity, confidence, and purpose.

About the Book

Work Signals helps professionals decode the unwritten rules of the workplace, understand the dynamics that shape their careers, and make better decisions with confidence. Whether you're early in your career or navigating leadership, this book provides a clear framework for understanding how work really works.

About the Authors

Ruchin Kansal

Ruchin is a seasoned strategist and educator with decades of experience helping organizations navigate transformation, leadership, and innovation. His work focuses on empowering professionals to understand the deeper signals that shape their careers.

Anthony W. Caputo

Anthony is a consultant and advisor with expertise in organizational behavior, leadership development, and workforce strategy. He brings a practical, grounded perspective to the challenges professionals face today.
